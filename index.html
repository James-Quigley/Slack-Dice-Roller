D&D Dice Roller for Slack

Even statistical distribution

Just like rolling fair dice.

Fast and easy syntax

/roll 3d6 [reason]

Examples

Roll 1 twenty sided die

/roll 1d20 dex save

Roll 2 six sided dice

/roll 2d6

Roll 10 four sided dice

/roll 10d4
